Welcome! This is a general academic website template for sharing research, teaching, and professional activities.

## Research Overview

This site is structured to support a broad range of academic disciplines. It can be used to present:

- Current research projects and long-term agenda
- Publications (journal articles, book chapters, working papers, and preprints)
- Ongoing collaborations and data or code resources
- Invited talks, conference presentations, and workshops

## Teaching

You can use the teaching section to describe:

- Courses currently offered
- Past courses and syllabi
- Teaching philosophy and methods
- Advising and mentorship

## Professional Information

The website also includes space for:

- Curriculum vitae
- Awards and grants
- Service and leadership roles
- Contact details and institutional affiliation

## Featured Work

Use this section to highlight one to three representative projects. A suggested format:

### Project Title
**Co-authors** | *Status (Published / Forthcoming / Working Paper)*

Briefly summarize the core question, method, and contribution in 3-5 sentences, and include links to the paper, slides, code, or replication materials.

To customize this site, update the profile in `_config.yml`, add publication markdown files in `_publications/`, and edit the pages in `_pages/`.
