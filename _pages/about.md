Kenneth is an Assistant Professor at the NUS Faculty of Law. He graduated from NUS (2014) with an LL.B. (First Class Honours) and a B.Soc.Sci. (Economics) (First Class Honours); the London School of Economics (2018, M.Sc. Economics); and Yale Law School (2019, LL.M.; 2024, J.S.D.). In 2022 he was a research fellow at Harvard Law School's Program on Corporate Governance. His research and teaching lie at the intersection of Law and Economics, especially Corporate Law, Mergers and Acquisitions, Securities Regulation, and Competition Law.
